Private Equity Advisory

Strategic Growth Capital & Liquidity Solutions

Our Private Equity practice is designed not just to raise capital, but to onboard the right strategic partners who align with the company's long-term vision. We act as the bridge between high-growth enterprises and the global institutional investment community, managing the entire transaction lifecycle from deal structuring to final closure.

Growth Capital (Minority Sale)

For companies seeking capital to fuel expansion while retaining operational control. We structure minority equity raises that balance capital infusion with governance autonomy.

Thesis Matching

Rigorous screening of PE funds & Family Offices to identify investors whose sector focus directly enhances your value proposition.

Investment Readiness

Pressure-testing the business model, preparing "Quality of Earnings" (QoE) assessments, and populating institutional-grade VDRs.

Deal Structuring

Negotiating critical terms: Board representation, voting rights, and anti-dilution ratification to protect founder interests.

Exit Horizon

Ensuring the incoming investor’s fund life matches your liquidity roadmap (e.g., IPO timeline), preventing forced-exit pressure.

Control Transactions (Majority Sale & Buyouts)

For founders looking to monetize wealth or divest a controlling interest. We manage complex change-of-control transactions with sensitivity and precision.

Partner Stewardship

A majority sale is a marriage. We identify buyers with a track record of stewarding legacy brands and retaining key management talent.

Valuation Maximization

We utilize a "competitive tension" bidding process to maximize Enterprise Value, compensating founders for platform value, not just historical earnings.

Transition & Governance

Structuring the post-transaction framework, defining rights and earn-out structures to ensure a seamless leadership transition.

Strategic Rollover

We advise on "Rollover Equity", allowing founders to retain a minority stake and participate in the "second bite of the apple" upon exit.

Secondary Transactions & Liquidity

Cap Table Rationalization

We assist companies in cleaning up their capitalization table by facilitating secondary sales—allowing early angel investors or ESOP holders to exit by selling their stakes to incoming institutional funds without diluting the primary share capital.

The Deal Execution Lifecycle

Our standard banking workflow for seamless execution

Preparation Phase

Business valuation, "Equity Story" crafting, and Teaser/IM preparation.

Marketing Phase

Roadshows with curated investors and management presentations.

Due Diligence

Management of VDR and coordination of legal/financial Q&A.

Closing Phase

Negotiation of SSA/SHA and condition precedent (CP) completion.
