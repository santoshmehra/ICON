How We Protect Capital

4 Guardrails + Early Warning System + Downside Playbook

01

Concentration Limits

No single company gets more than 15% of fund capital. Forces diversification.

02

Valuation Discipline

Entry at ₹500-2,000 Cr sweet spot. We target 12-24 months from IPO—not seed, not post-listing.

Financial Covenants
