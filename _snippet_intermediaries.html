Intermediaries in IPO Process

Professional Groups Engaged for a Successful IPO

7
Key Players
1

BRLM

Merchant Bankers who divide the function of being Lead, Post Issue compliance & Roadshows

2

Legal Attorneys

1 Legal team is required for preparation of DRHP while the other is necessary for due diligence. International Counsel may be necessary in certain cases

3

PR Agency

To run the SEBI Mandated paper advertisements, to enable roadshows for retail investors, press conferences etc.

4

RTA

Responsible for DMAT of securities & also Allotment of shares upon completion of IPO

5

Industry Reports

They are usually prepared by professional agencies which prepare an essential part of the DRHP

6

Sponsor Bank

Bankers to the issue is an essential intermediary who enables the escrow account for IPO Proceeds

7

Auditors

A Peer-reviewed Auditor with proficiency in IPOs & IND AS financial statements

Amatya Advisors coordinates with all intermediaries to ensure seamless IPO execution
