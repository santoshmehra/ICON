Private Equity & M&A Advisory

We assist companies in raising growth capital by matching them with investors whose thesis aligns with their long-term vision.

Minority Stake Sale

  • Targeting: We screen investors based on their past track record, industry focus, and potential portfolio synergies.
  • Readiness: We prepare companies for "Technical & Financial" due diligence by organizing records and governance structures.
  • Deal Structuring: We negotiate critical terms including valuation, board representation, information rights, and exit timelines.

Majority Stake / Buyout

  • Partner Selection: For promoters looking to divest a controlling stake, we identify institutional buyers with the operational expertise to scale the business further.
  • Negotiation: We focus on securing the best valuation while clearly defining the rights and responsibilities of the incoming majority partner.
  • Strategic Exit: We structure the deal to include options for the remaining minority stake, ensuring a complete liquidity roadmap for the exiting management.

Private Debt & Credit Solutions

For companies seeking non-dilutive capital or alternative financing structures, our Private Debt practice offers bespoke credit solutions. This serves as a critical bridge for companies that wish to avoid equity dilution or need specialized capital that traditional bank loans cannot provide.

Venture Debt

We facilitate debt raising for high-growth startups to extend their cash runway between equity rounds. This minimizes dilution for founders while providing the capital needed to hit valuation milestones.

Mezzanine Financing

We structure hybrid debt-equity instruments that sit between senior debt and equity. This is often used to fund acquisitions or buyouts where senior lenders have reached their limits.

Structured Credit & Acquisition Financing

We assist in raising capital for special situations, such as promoter financing, acquisition funding, or bridge loans leading up to an IPO.

Yield & Terms

We leverage our network of credit funds and NBFCs to negotiate competitive interest rates and covenants, ensuring the cost of capital is optimized against the company's cash flow capabilities.
